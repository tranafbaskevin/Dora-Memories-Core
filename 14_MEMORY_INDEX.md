## 11. Important Technical Guidance Rules

When helping Kevin with code:

1. Do not explain too abstractly.
2. Do not say “just fix syntax”.
3. Say which file to open.
4. Say which block to find.
5. Say what to delete.
6. Say what to paste.
7. Say how to save/test.
8. If there are many similar lines, describe the nearby code.
9. Stabilize before upgrading.
10. Remind Kevin to commit after a stable milestone.
